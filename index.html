Lawns

Green lawns

A lovely green lawn is an excellent example of something a homeowner can change to help their home be more eco-friendly. Grass, in some areas, requires watering to be healthy. By replacing a yard with wildflowers or similar, your yard will need less care and help will promote bees and other pollinators.

Solar

The benfits of green energy

Solar is a great way to start really making your house green. By installing a solar system, you can start saving money on your power. In some places you can also sell your extra power back to the power company. It’s a great way to set your house up for being off the grid.

Rain Collection

Rain collection

Collecting the rain that falls is a great way to recapture some of the lost water, saving it for when you need it. It can be helpful for anything from watering plants, all the way to cleaning around your yard. Rain collection can be as simple as a barrel under your downspout to something more advance.
