About us

Founded in the early 2000s, our is to help homeowners turn their house more environmentally friendly. After doing the research, we figured out the best way to do this would be to both offer services to the homeowner that do not have the time to do it themselves, but also offer guidance for those that do have the time to do it themselves. We believe that even if we are not making money on every service we offer, it is for the greater good, as we are helping someone help the plant in a small way. We believe that nature should be celebrated, as it is beautifully, and is a great way to really set your house apart from all others.
